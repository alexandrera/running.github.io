Road Running

The sport

Road running is everything.

I love running.

Bellow we have a list of some benefits of practicing it according to wikipedia:

  • Weight loss
  • Improved cardiovascular and respiratory health
  • Reduced total blood cholesterol
  • Strengthening of bones
  • It helps to improves psychological well-being
That said, after you start running on a regular basis you will find that your shoe is not enough and then you will look for some upgrade. There are a lot of brands with very nice technologies like Nike, Adidas, Brooks, Saucony, Mizuno, Fila, etc.
